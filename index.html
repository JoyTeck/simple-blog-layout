Tteokbokki

Posted on February 20, 2023

This firery dish features chewy rice cakes (garaetteok) seasoned with spicy chile paste. A staple of Korean street food, you will often find bubbling pots of tteokbokki ready to serve. Garnish with sesame seeds for an extra crunch.
